Tactical Ability: Whistler
Shoot a projectile that heats up an enemy’s gun as they shoot, overheating the weapon and causing 
damage.




Passive Ability: Sling
Store a third weapon in the Sling, which you can access via inventory or Character Utility Action.





Ultimate Ability: Tempest
Give nearby teammates faster reloads, faster armed movement speed, and infinite ammo while Tempest 
is active. Ballistic’s Sling weapon will also be equipped and upgraded to gold.
