Tactical Ability: Piercing Spikes
Throw out a patch of ferrofluid which turns into spikes when enemies are near. Catalyst is immune 
to enemy spikes.




Passive Ability: Barricade
Reinforce doors to keep enemies out. Spaces where doors have been destroyed can also be reinforced.





Ultimate Ability: Dark Veil
Raise a permeable wall of ferrofluid. Enemies who walk through it will be temporarily slowed down 
and blinded.
